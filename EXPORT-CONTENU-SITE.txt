- Recouvrement : Creances debiteurs et AGRASC

[02 — Assurance Vie et Capitalisation]

Historiquement specialise dans les contrats d'assurance vie et de capitalisation, le cabinet vous assiste sur :

- Recherche de contrats : Identification, acces aux informations, droits des beneficiaires
- Analyse des clauses : Validite, impact sur les droits successoraux
- Actions en justice : Reduction, rapport, nullite en cas de primes excessives
- Contrats de capitalisation : Perte ou vol de bons au porteur, recuperation de valeur financiere, action en paiement de la valeur de rachat

[SECTION — A Strategic French-American Law Firm]

For over 35 years, Cabinet Fouere has been at the forefront of insurance law and cross-border asset recovery in France. Founded in 1987 by Maitre Jacques Fouere, member of the Paris Bar, our firm provides fully bilingual legal counsel (French and English) to American clients navigating the complexities of the French legal system.

Whether you are an heir seeking to recover a life insurance policy, a family dealing with a French succession, or a business resolving a complex insurance dispute, our firm offers the strategic depth and cultural fluency required to protect your interests effectively.

We understand that dealing with legal matters in a foreign country can be daunting. That is precisely why we have built our practice around clarity, precision and direct communication — in your language, on your schedule.

---

[SECTION — Inheritance & Life Insurance]
(Successions & Assurance Vie)

France's succession laws differ significantly from those in the United States. Forced heirship rules, complex tax treaties and administrative procedures in French can create significant barriers for American heirs.

Our firm specializes in:
- Locating life insurance policies — Identifying and recovering unclaimed contracts held by French insurance companies, using official registries and legal procedures.
- Succession planning & disputes — Navigating French forced heirship rules, coordinating with notaires, and resolving disputes between heirs across jurisdictions.
- Clause analysis & beneficiary rights — Reviewing contract terms, validating beneficiary designations, and challenging excessive premiums when warranted.
- Tax coordination — Working with French fiscal authorities and US tax advisors to ensure compliance with both jurisdictions.

---

[SECTION — Cross-Border Asset Recovery]
(Recuperation d'actifs bancaires & immobiliers)

Many American families discover — sometimes decades later — that a relative held bank accounts, investment portfolios or real estate in France. Recovering these assets requires navigating a maze of French administrative, legal and fiscal procedures.

Cabinet Fouere provides end-to-end assistance:
- Bank account recovery — Engaging with French banks, the Caisse des Depots, and CICLADE to locate and release dormant funds.
- Real estate identification — Searching the cadastre and land registry (conservation des hypotheques) to locate properties and establish ownership chains.
- Insurance policy claims — Filing claims with French insurers, challenging unjustified denials, and pursuing legal action when necessary.
- Administrative coordination — Handling all interactions with French authorities on your behalf, eliminating the language barrier entirely.

---

[SECTION — Litigation & Negotiation]
(Contentieux & negociations)

When amicable resolution is not possible, Cabinet Fouere provides robust litigation support before French courts. Our three decades of courtroom experience in insurance disputes give our clients a decisive advantage.

- Pre-litigation strategy — Assessing the strength of your case, identifying leverage points, and preparing a compelling legal strategy before engaging in formal proceedings.
- Court representation — Full advocacy before French civil and commercial courts, with bilingual reporting at every stage.
- Amicable negotiation — Engaging directly with opposing parties and their counsel to reach favorable settlements without the cost and delay of trial.

---

[SECTION — Administrative & Tax Support]
(Accompagnement administratif & fiscal)

The French administrative landscape can be overwhelming for foreign clients. Our firm serves as your single point of contact in France, managing all correspondence, filings and official interactions on your behalf.

- Tax declarations — Preparing and filing French tax declarations related to inherited assets, in coordination with the Direction Generale des Finances Publiques.
- Cross-border tax optimization — Leveraging the France-US tax treaty to minimize double taxation and ensure full compliance.
- Document procurement — Obtaining official French documents (actes de notoriete, certificats de propriete) and coordinating apostille procedures.
- Ongoing advisory — Providing continuous legal guidance throughout the entire process, from initial assessment to final resolution.

---

[02 — Life Insurance & Capitalisation]

Historically specialised in life insurance and capitalisation contracts, the firm assists you with:

- Policy Tracing: Identification, information access, beneficiary rights
- Clause Analysis: Validity, impact on inheritance rights
- Legal Action: Reduction, reporting, nullity in cases of excessive premiums
- Capitalisation Contracts: Lost or stolen bearer bonds, financial value recovery, surrender value claims
