The Founder

JF

Maître Jacques Fouéré

Member of the Paris Bar since 1987

Maître Jacques Fouéré founded the firm in 1987 after practising within American business law firms in Paris. This international experience shaped a rigorous and pragmatic approach to litigation, rooted in the most demanding standards.

For nearly four decades, he has dedicated his practice to complex disputes in insurance, financial intermediary liability and asset fraud. His expertise spans the full range of life insurance litigation, capitalisation contracts, professional liability and fund misappropriation.
