Banking Procedures in Estate Matters

The firm assists foreign clients with procedures involving French banking institutions when a person passes away in France and leaves financial assets.

Identification

Identification of bank accounts, insurance contracts and financial assets held in France.

Communication

Exchanges with banks and insurers to obtain the information required to release the funds.

Documentation

Gathering of required documents: death certificate, certificate of heirship, identity documents.

Filing

Filing of the estate tax return with the French tax authorities.
